Evidence gap map for information and government accountability

We (MIT GOV/LAB) designed a systematic search for literature published in the last 10 years addressing the effects of information on government accountability, both between government and citizens and within government. A total of 34 relevant papers were found. The table below maps the findings of the evidence base. An accompanying report reviews the literature and proposes a framework for thinking of the chain between information and accountability.
